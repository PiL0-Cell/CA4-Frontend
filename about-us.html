About DrukFlix

🎯

Our Mission

DrukFlix is dedicated to preserving and promoting Bhutanese cinema by making award-winning films accessible to audiences worldwide. We believe in the power of storytelling to bridge cultures and share the unique perspective of the Land of the Thunder Dragon.

📖

Our Story

Founded in 2026, DrukFlix emerged from a simple idea: to create a dedicated platform where Bhutanese films could reach a global audience. What started as a small collection has grown into a curated library of the finest Bhutanese cinema, from award-winning international sensations to hidden gems.

We work directly with filmmakers to bring you authentic, high-quality streaming experiences that honor the art of Bhutanese storytelling.
