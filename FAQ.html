Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DrukFlix

DrukFlix is a streaming service dedicated to Bhutanese cinema. We offer a curated collection of Bhutanese films, from award-winning international sensations to independent gems, all in one place.
DrukFlix offers both free and premium content. Some films are available to watch for free, while others require a subscription. Check our pricing page for more details on subscription plans.
You can watch DrukFlix on any device with a web browser including desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. We support Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge browsers.
Use the search bar in the navigation menu to search by movie title, genre, or year. You can also browse movies by genre using the filter tags on the Movies page.
Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the page. Fill in your name, email address, and create a password. Once registered, you can log in and start watching!
Log into your account, go to the "Change Password" page from the navigation menu or your profile dropdown. Enter your current password and your new password, then confirm the change.
DrukFlix features a growing collection of Bhutanese films including "Lunana: A Yak in the Classroom", "The Monk and the Gun", "Honeygiver Among the Dogs", and many more across various genres like Drama, Comedy, Horror, Romance, and Thriller.
